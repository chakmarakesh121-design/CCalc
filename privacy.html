Privacy Policy

Your privacy is protected on ContractorsCalc.

We collect only the data needed to operate this website and provide a smooth construction estimating experience. Your calculator entries stay private and are not shared without consent.

Information we collect

ContractorsCalc does not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it, such as submitting a support request or contacting us directly. Calculator inputs are processed locally in your browser and are not stored by the site.

How we use data

Data submitted through contact or feedback forms is used only to respond to your requests and improve the platform. We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties.

Cookies and analytics

We use minimal cookies or session storage only where necessary for site functionality and navigation. No tracking or third-party analytics are used unless explicitly added in future updates.

Security practices

Our site is built with standard web security practices to protect your use of the calculators and preserve data integrity. We recommend using an up-to-date browser for the safest experience.

Third-party services

If AI assistance or backend integrations are enabled, those services may collect minimal usage data according to their own policies. We do not share your private calculator entries without clear consent.

Policy updates

We may update this privacy policy over time. Changes will be posted on this page so you always know how your information is handled.
